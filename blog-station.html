Generative Models : The next machine learning boom ?
 Deep generative models have seen a resurgence in popularity in recent years. Auto-regressive networks (e.g., pixelRNNs,
RNN language models), variational autoencoders (VAEs), and many of their variants and expansions are emerging techni-
ques that have produced outstanding results in a variety of applications. In areas like creating realistic, high-resolution
photos, editing and modifying text, learning interpretable data representations, automatically enhancing data for other
model training, etc., researchers are making remarkable strides.
