PROJECTS

MNIST Digit Recognizer: Recognize Handwritten Digits
 Introduction : MNIST ("Modified National Institute of Standards and Technology") is the de facto “hello world” dataset
of computer vision. In this project, your goal is to correctly identify digits from a dataset of tens of thousands of
handwritten images. Experimented with different algorithms to learn first-hand what works well and how techniques
compare.
 Tools used: Python, Deep learning, Computer vision Kaggle competition : Digit Recognizer

Cycle - GANs : Turn images to Monet style images
 Introduction : A GAN consists of at least two neural networks: a generator model and a discriminator model. The
generator is a neural network that creates the images. In this project, you should generate images in the style of
Monet. This generator is trained using a discriminator. The two models will work against each other, with the generator
trying to trick the discriminator, and the discriminator trying to accurately classify the real vs. generated images.
Our task is to build a GAN that generates 7,000 to 10,000 Monet-style images.
 Tools used: Python, Deep Learning, Adversarial Networks Kaggle competition : I’m Something of a Painter Myself

Exo-Planet Detection : Predict using Light Flux intensity
 Introduction : Exoplanets are planets beyond our own solar system. Thousands have been discovered in the past two
decades, mostly with NASA’s Kepler Space Telescope. In this project various Machine learning models have been
implemented to predict if it is a exo-planet on basis of light flux intensities. Data processing and anaslysis is also been
performed for a better understand of dataset.
 Tools used: Python, Statistics, Machine Learning, EDA
